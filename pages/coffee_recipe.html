Coffee Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of ground coffee
  • Sugar or sweetener (optional)
  • Milk or cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the water in a kettle or pot.
  2. Add the ground coffee to a coffee maker or French press.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the coffee grounds.
  4. Let it brew for about 4-5 minutes.
  5. Press the plunger down if using a French press, or remove the coffee filter if using a drip coffee maker.
  6. Pour the brewed coffee into a cup.
  7. Add sugar or sweetener to taste, if desired.
  8. Add milk or cream, if desired.
  9. Stir well and enjoy your freshly brewed coffee!
